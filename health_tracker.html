💡 Health Awareness Tips
🥗
Balanced Diet
Fill half your plate with vegetables. Choose whole grains and lean proteins to maintain energy and heart health.
🚶
Stay Active
Aim for 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity per week. Even daily 30-minute walks make a huge difference.
💧
Stay Hydrated
Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration supports kidney function, digestion, and energy levels.
😴
Quality Sleep
7–9 hours of sleep is essential. Poor sleep increases risk of heart disease, obesity and diabetes significantly.
🧘
Manage Stress
Chronic stress damages your heart. Practice mindfulness, deep breathing, or yoga to reduce cortisol levels.
🩺
Regular Check-ups
Annual health screenings detect issues early. Don't wait for symptoms — prevention is always better than cure.
